Designing the world’s largest branded content catalog: Discover

Falcon.io acquired Unmetric in October 2019 and decided to integrate Unmetric’s two core products — Analyze and Discover — into Falcon.io platform and call it Benchmark. Our team decided to split this huge integration effort into three parts.

The first phase will allow new Benchmark users to access the Unmetric platform both from inside the Falcon platform by clicking the Benchmark icon in the sidebar, and also from the Unmetric sign-in screen by clicking the “Sign in with Falcon.io” single sign-on button.

  • Wireframming

Discovery

Our head of product shared a doc outlining product goals, specifications and MVP requirements. I got on a call with him to discuss what business outcomes we are trying to achieve and what business outcomes are we looking forward to with launching Discover. I also added assumptions we were making as a business and on users behalf. We agreed that if these assumptions are proven to be false, our product might fail. We also listed down the least amount of feature that can help our target audience to achieve their outcomes.

After outlining outcomes and assumptions for both the business and user, I initiated discussion around user needs and challenges they are facing. I asked questions around efforts they are making right now to meet their needs and get over their challenges.
