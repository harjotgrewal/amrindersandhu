Relpost – Handpicked related posts for Web Designers

2010–2011 • Side Project

Whenever I was researching or learning some specific topics, the main problem I faced was that there was no way to find useful related articles on a subject. This led me to create Relpost. I designed the website and curated the content every week. My wife – Harjot, who is a front-end developer – coded the site and also handled the Wordpress setup.
