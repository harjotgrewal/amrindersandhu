    Mishriwala to Ancaster


    1983–1999

    It’s this very small village in Punjab, India where Amrinder was raised while learning family values and fundamentals of being a good human being. The tiny bit of creativity he has, he owes it to his mother, and like every child ever born, he is indebted to his parents for this life.


    2000–2008

    A lot happened in those 8 years. He moved out to live in 4 different cities, managed to pass secondary school and finally graduation, appeared in various placement tests and finally got selected at Wipro (which he didn’t join), played soccer at inter-varsity level (big deal), came up 2nd in cross-country race (his first and last), played cricket and took a shot at weight-lifting and swimming (failed at both), met his wife, got engaged and finally got married.


    2009–Present

    After dropping everything he learned (academically), he pursued a career in design. Starting full-time freelancing in Feb 2008, Amrinder has never looked back (at C++ or Wipro). He started designing and coding small websites for local clients and moved globally and worked with over a dozen of customers spanning 6 continents. From designing logos, visiting cards, letterheads, pamphlets, newsletters, decks to websites and applications, he got the opportunity to work on everything design from scratch while collaborating cross-functionally, improving and introducing processes, frameworks and tools.
