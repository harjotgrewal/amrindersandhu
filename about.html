Currently, I’m the Head of Design at Unmetric, where I have been working from day 1 and designed all the core products from scratch.

Prior to this, I have lead design at various early-stage startups designing everything from products, websites, logos, pitch decks, newsletters, business cards and videos.

My clients include Intel, Zomato, Grasshopper, CIBC, MyEd, ProProfs, and PCL. I got job offers from Google, Groupon, and Intel that I couldn’t accept because of relocation.

In 2007, I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. I grew up in a small village (Mishriwala) in Punjab, India. I’m based in Ancaster, Canada

I designed my first website (about Arnold Schwarzenegger) in 2003 using Microsoft FrontPage. Before that I used to draw and paint brand logos to perfection. Later, I started playing with Photoshop which helped me later when I started designing full time in 2008.

My interests includes walking, cooking, watching movies, building DIY stuff, playing cards with my kids, and reading design books. I love simplicity, nature, rain, mountains, and solitude.

Besides everything stated above, I’m a superhero for my kids and a hero for my wife.
